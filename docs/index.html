Training Module

The Training Module is designed to educate Nodal Officers and Department Officials on the grievance application process, ensuring seamless handling and resolution of public grievances. This module provides step-by-step guidance on application registration, documentation, forwarding procedures, tracking mechanisms, and best practices for efficient grievance redressal. Interactive resources, instructional materials, and real-time case studies enhance understanding, enabling officials to deliver transparent, timely, and effective public service.
