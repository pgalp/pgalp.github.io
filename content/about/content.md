The **Alappuzha District Collector’s Online Public Grievance System (സ്നേഹപൂർവ്വം കളക്ടർ )** is an initiative by the district administration, developed with the support of **KSITM Alappuzha**, to streamline the grievance redressal process. This digital platform enables residents to submit grievances and track their resolution status online, ensuring transparency and efficiency. By leveraging technology, the system enhances public service delivery, allowing authorities to address issues more effectively and improve governance in the district.
